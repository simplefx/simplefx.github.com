The SimpleExpression Object

A representation of criteria used by Find and FindAll to describe filters more complex than simple equality as assumed in All, Get, FindBy and FindAllBy.

For example

db.Albums.GenreId == 1

Individual expressions can use a number of different operators

  • Arithmetic
  • Mathematical
  • IN, BETWEEN, LIKE, IS NULL, IS NOT NULL

Individual expressions can be concatenated together with && or & to denote “AND”, and || or | to denote “OR”. Parentheses for altering grouping of expressions are supported.

Read also the Column Selection page for more on how to identify the columns in a SimpleExpression.
